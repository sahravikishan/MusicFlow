MusicFlow

Create, Learn, and Master Guitar Music

MusicFlow is a comprehensive guitar learning and composition platform that combines real-time visualization, intelligent analysis, and high-quality audio playback. Whether you're a beginner learning your first chords or an advanced player composing complex pieces, MusicFlow provides all the tools you need in one beautiful interface.

Real-time Fretboard

Experience the most intuitive guitar fretboard visualization available. See exactly where to place your fingers with our color-coded system.

  • Interactive fretboard display
  • Color-coded note positions
  • Support for all guitar types
  • Zoom and pan controls
  • Note name overlays

Audio Playback

Listen to your compositions with studio-quality audio. Multiple guitar tones and effects bring your music to life.

  • Studio-quality sound engine
  • Multiple guitar tone presets
  • Adjustable tempo and pitch
  • Loop playback support
  • Export to audio files

Chord Detection

Automatically detect and analyze chords from your playing. Get instant feedback on progressions and harmonic structure.

  • Automatic chord recognition
  • Chord progression analysis
  • Alternative fingering suggestions
  • Comprehensive chord library
  • Custom chord creation
