Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MusicFlow collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other laws. The Service is a prototype; data handling is for development purposes.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

  • Personal Identifiers: Username, email, password (hashed) during signup/login.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser info, music generation logs.
  • Generated Content: Metadata from MIDI/WAV/MP3 files stored in media/.

No sensitive data or children's data (under 13) without consent.

3. How Do We Collect Data?

Via forms (signup.html, login.html), cookies for sessions, and logs during music generation. No third-party trackers in prototype.

4. How Do We Use Your Data?

  • Authentication: Verify logins and send password reset emails.
  • Service Provision: Generate and store music files.
  • Improvement: Analyze usage (anonymized) for debugging.

Retention: Data kept while account active; deleted on request.

5. How Do We Share Your Data?

No sharing or selling. Only for legal requirements or with consent. Stored in SQLite3 locally.

6. Data Security

Uses Django CSRF protection, hashing for passwords. Breaches notified within 72 hours (GDPR).

7. Your Data Rights

  • Access, correct, delete, or export data.
  • Opt-out of processing; revoke consent.
  • CCPA: No discrimination for rights exercise.

Contact us to exercise rights.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Uses session cookies for auth. Manage via browser.

9. Children's Privacy

Not for under 13. Parental consent required if applicable (COPPA).

10. Changes to This Policy

Updates posted here. Continued use accepts changes.
